Privacy Policy

This application collects and stores users’ phone numbers solely for the purpose of enabling account authentication and in-app SMS-based communication between registered users.

We do not sell, share, or distribute user data to third parties.

SMS messages are used for account verification, notifications, and communication between users who have explicitly opted in by registering and verifying their phone number.

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP or by updating their account settings. Standard message and data rates may apply.
